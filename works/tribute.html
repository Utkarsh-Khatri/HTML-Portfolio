Sanduk Ruit

Here is a timeline of Dr. Sanduk Ruit's life

  • 1954 -born in Wolang Chun Gola,Taplejung in Nepal.
  • 1969 - Graduated from Siddhartha Vanasthali School.
  • 1976 - Studied MBBS on scholarship at King George's Medical College, Lucknow.
  • 1981 - Acheived Master's Degree in opthamology at All India Institue of Medical Science, Delhi.
  • 1984 - Worked at Eye Hospital in Tripureshwor, Kathmandu for eight years.
  • 1986 - Australian Opthamologist Fred Hollows offered him further study about Cataract surgery at Australia.
  • 1987 - Married to Nanda Shrestha, an opthalmic nurse.
  • 1995 - Developed and Intraocular lens that could be produced far more cheaply and was more effective in treating people infected with Cataract.
  • 2006 - Awarded with Roman Magsaysay Award for international understanding.
  • 2007 - Awarded with Asian of the year
  • 2018 - Government of India awarded him with Padma Shri Award.
  • 2023 - Awarded with ISA award for service to humanity.

"When I am operating, my soul, my heart, my brain, everything is in the eye. Because I don't think about anything else, just the eye."

--Sanduk Ruit
